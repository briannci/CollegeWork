Tenerife is also one of the Canary Islands. It is the most populated of all seven Canary Islands and also the highest elevation of Spain.

Tenerife offers beautiful weather alongside its elevated nature. It has one of the biggest carnivals in the world.

Tenerife has severeal beautiful golden sandy beaches including the popular Costa Adeje and Playa de las Americas.
