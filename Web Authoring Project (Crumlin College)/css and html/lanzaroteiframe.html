Lanzarote is one of the Canary Islands which is most easterly.

The island is 37 miles in length. It offers beautiful weather thoughout the year. The summer months are the busiest time of the year here with over 30,000 visitors weekly from all over the world.

Lanzarote has several beaches, but two of the most popular are Playa Blanca and Papagayo. Lazarote's weather reaches up to 30°C during its peak months.

  • Timanfaya National Park
  • Markets
  • The Cactus Gardens
  • El Golfo
