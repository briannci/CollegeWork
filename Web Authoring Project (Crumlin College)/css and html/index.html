Benidorm is located 45km northeast of Alicante.

It has mild winters and very hot summers.

The mountains surrounding the Costa Blanca protect Benidorm from southernly winds which means Benidorm will be very warm throughout the year reaching up to 38°C.

Click here for more about Benidorm.

Ibiza is one of the Balearic Islands.

It is hugely popular with young tourists who want a lively holiday in the sun!

Ibiza has 7 beaches in total, including the popular Talamanca beach. Ibiza's weather reaches up to 27°C during July - August.

Click here for more about Ibiza.

Lanzarote is a popular destinaton for everyone but in particular, couples!

It is situated off the coast of Africa. It is the most easterly of the Canary Islands.

Lanzarote offers a mild dry climate averaging 21°C in January to 29°C in August

Click here for more about Lanzarote.

Tenerife is a very popular destination when it comes to sun holidays!

If your looking for the party scene look no further than Playa de las Americas.

The average temperatures are 20-22°C. In the hottest summer months it reaches up to 30°C.
