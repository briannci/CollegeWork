Ibiza, The Balearics, is located off the coast of the city of Valencia in Spain.

Ibiza has 7 beaches which include the popular Talamanca beach. Talamanca Beach is a curved sandy beach located next to Ibiza Town. Ibiza is a white island with cove beaches, beautiful palm trees and high cliffs.

It is worldwide reknown for its party atmosphere!

Ibiza enjoys mild weather throughout the winter and hot weather throughout the summer, reaching up to 30 degrees in summer months

Ibiza Town and the Port area is only 15 minutes walk from the beach.

Attractions:

  • Water Sports
  • Mountain Bike Tours
  • Island Tours
  • Talamanca Beach
  • Boat Charter
  • Sightseeing Tours
  • Nightlife
