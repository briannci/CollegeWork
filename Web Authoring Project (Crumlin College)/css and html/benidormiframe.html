Benidorm, Alicante, Costa Blanca, Spain

About Benidorm

Benidorm is located 45km northeast of Alicante. It is one of the main attractions among the Costa Blanca. Benidorm has two main beaches which are the Levante and the Poniente.

It reaches 40 degrees in August! It gets so hot in the summer months because of the surrounding mountains of Alicante which stops southernly winds from reaching Benidorm.

  • Poniente Beach
